Garden Salad

Easy to make recipe for a lazy weekend afternoon

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g new potatoes
  • 350g runner beans
  • 1 bunch of spring onions
  • 240g sunblushed\sundried tomatoes, drained
  • 200g Wensleydale cheese
  • 15g fresh mint leaves
  • 4-5 tbsp honey and mustard dressing

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Slice the potatoes and cook them in a large pan of salted boiling water for 7 minutes. While theyâ€™re cooking, trim and diagonally slice the runner beans and chop the spring onions. Add the beans to the pan and cook for a further 7-9 minutes, until the potatoes and beans are just tender.

  2. Drain the veg and run under cold water to halt the cooking. Shake the colander to get rid of as much water as possible, then tip into a large bowl. Add the onions and tomatoes, crumble in the cheese and mix well.

  3. Add most of the mint and dressing and toss everything lightly together. Tip into a serving dish, drizzle with a little more dressing and scatter over the rest of the mint.
